Fast, High-Fidelity Video Editing

A video editing model that turns a short text prompt into a high-fidelity edit — clips up to 10 seconds, results in under 30 seconds. It handles everything from precise local edits to scene-wide transformations, and composite requests that combine several edits at once.

Curated by Mannat Singh — every example below was generated with the publicly available feature on Meta AI and Instagram Edits, as of May 2026.

Erase unwanted items or people from your video to create a clean, focused, or private scene.

👗 Changing fashion and wardrobe

Alter the clothing, style, make-up, or accessories of characters in a video.

🌄 Changing lighting, environment or backgrounds

Transform the mood and setting of your video by modifying the lighting, changing the backdrop, or adjusting the overall environment.

💥 Visual effects

Introduce a wide array of dynamic visual enhancements, animations, or post-production effects to make your videos more engaging and fun.

🎨 Style changes

Apply high-level artistic or thematic changes, giving your video a completely new, stylized look.

🧩 Composite edits

Perform multiple, complex edits simultaneously within a single request, allowing for sophisticated video transformations that combine several capabilities.

🎬 Changing actions

Modify the movements or activities of subjects in the video, enabling fine-tuned adjustments to how characters are behaving or interacting within the scene. Note that this is hit-or-miss and may not work well for various videos and/or prompts.
