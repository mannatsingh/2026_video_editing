✨ Adding objects or characters

Seamlessly introduce new elements, props, or characters into a scene.

🔄 Replacing objects or characters

Swap out existing elements in a video — from replacing a specific object with another, to changing an entire character.

🗑️ Removing objects or characters

Erase unwanted items or people from your video to create a clean, focused, or private scene.

👗 Changing fashion and wardrobe

Alter the clothing, style, make-up, or accessories of characters in a video.

🌄 Changing lighting, environment or backgrounds

Transform the mood and setting of your video by modifying the lighting, changing the backdrop, or adjusting the overall environment.

💥 Visual effects

Introduce a wide array of dynamic visual enhancements, animations, or post-production effects to make your videos more engaging and fun.

🎨 Style changes

Apply high-level artistic or thematic changes, giving your video a completely new, stylized look.

🧩 Composite edits

Perform multiple, complex edits simultaneously within a single request, allowing for sophisticated video transformations that combine several capabilities.

🎬 Changing actions

Modify the movements or activities of subjects in the video, enabling fine-tuned adjustments to how characters are behaving or interacting within the scene. Note that this is hit-or-miss and may not work well for various videos and/or prompts.

Limitations

The model is not audio-aware, it struggles with small faces and fast motion, and it is not a thinking model. Changing actions is very cool when it works, but it can also generate morphed or messed-up videos.

Despite the limitations, there are a lot of fun, exciting, and useful edits that can be made with the model — especially given the fast turnaround time.

Behind the launch

This model was built last year by a fantastic team of researchers, and launched this year with amazing product folks through several iterations and experiments — with big contributions from the safety, privacy, and legal teams.

A lot of work goes in after model training: deployment, inference optimization (latency driven down to near-realtime — the majority of the current latency is outside the backend call!), training-free quality fixes, and cross-functional collaboration.
